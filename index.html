Our divisions

Four rooms, one current.

Aurora isn't one kind of club wearing a startup label. It's four working rooms that share the same energy, people who'd rather build the thing than talk about building it.

Ideas Lab

Pitch a concept, get matched with a team, and build a working version of it. Ideas Lab is Aurora's startup incubator: mentorship, prototyping nights, and a demo day every term.

Debate Arena

Sharpen the argument, not just the opinion. We run practice rounds, prep for inter-school tournaments, and build the strongest possible case for whatever position you draw.

Chess Tournament

Everything just as realistic as a OTB tournament, timed, strategic and clean.

Wildcard

Got something that doesn't fit a category: a film, a campaign, an experiment? That's exactly what Wildcard exists for. If it's interesting, we'll find a way to do it.
